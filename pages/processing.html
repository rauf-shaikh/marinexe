Our step-by-step processing excellence

Follow the journey from fresh catch to export-ready seafood. Our six-stage process ensures every product meets the highest standards of freshness, hygiene, and quality before it reaches global markets with top-quality fish and seafood varieties.

01

Catch Sourcing

Partnering with certified fishermen across Indian coasts for fresh raw materials.

02

Preliminary Inspection

Initial quality checks conducted at collection centers with trained supervisors.

03

Cleaning & Sorting

Hygienic cleaning, size grading, and type-wise sorting at HACCP-certified plants.

04

Processing & Cutting

Portioning, descaling, deveining (for shrimp), and filleting using automated tools.

05

Freezing

IQF, Blast Freezing, and Plate Freezing techniques to lock freshness instantly.

06

Packing

Vacuum sealing and export-grade packaging under controlled conditions.
